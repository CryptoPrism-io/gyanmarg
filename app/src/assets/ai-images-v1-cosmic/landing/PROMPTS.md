# Landing Page Image Prompts

## Style Consistency
Landing page images are the most premium:
- Larger dimensions (1920x1080 for hero)
- Maximum visual impact
- Dark background (#0A0A0B)
- Amber gold (#F59E0B) dominant
- Can have subtle animation potential
