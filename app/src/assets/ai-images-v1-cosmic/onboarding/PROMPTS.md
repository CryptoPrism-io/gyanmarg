# Onboarding Slide Prompts

## Style Consistency Note
All three images should feel like a visual story progression:
1. Crisis/Problem (dark, dispersing)
2. Discovery/Hope (light emerging)
3. Transformation/Power (full radiance)

---

## onboarding-01-forgetting.png
**The Forgetting Crisis - "90% Lost"**

```
A human head silhouette dissolving into scattered particles and dust,
memories and knowledge fragments floating away into a dark void,
the fragments glow faintly with dying amber (#F59E0B) light,
cosmic neural minimalism style, dark background (#0A0A0B),
sense of loss and fading, ethereal and melancholic,
subtle purple (#8B5CF6) mist in the background,
cinematic dramatic lighting, 8k, ultra detailed
```

**Alt prompt (simpler):**
```
Abstract visualization of memory loss, glowing amber particles
dispersing into darkness, neural pathways fading and breaking apart,
dark void background, minimal and ethereal, cinematic lighting,
gold (#F59E0B) and purple (#8B5CF6) color accents on black (#0A0A0B)
```
